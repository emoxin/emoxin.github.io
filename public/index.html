Biography


I obtained my Ph.D. in Psychology from Tsinghua University. l am interested in affectiveneuroscience, affective disorders, and how to connect these two. Currently, l am undergoing postdoctoral training in Dr. Bylsma's lab, honing my skills in ambulatoryphysiological assessment to bridge the laboratory and real-life research findings.
I have two cockatiels, Lady Bo and Mr. Hui, who share one of my research interests: studying human emotions in real-life settings.
